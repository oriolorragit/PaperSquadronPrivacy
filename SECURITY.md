**Last updated:** July 15, 2026

## Privacy Policy for Paper Squadron

Voltix Studios ("we", "our", or "us") operates the mobile game **Paper Squadron**.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use our application.

### Information We Collect

The game itself does not collect personally identifiable information directly.

However, third-party services used by the app may collect certain information automatically, including:

* Device information
* Advertising identifiers
* IP address
* Approximate location
* App usage statistics
* Crash reports

### Advertising

Paper Squadron uses **Google AdMob** to display advertisements.

Google AdMob may collect and process data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

You can learn more here:

* https://policies.google.com/privacy
* https://support.google.com/admob

### Analytics

The app may use analytics services to improve performance and user experience. These services may collect anonymous usage information.

### Children's Privacy

Paper Squadron is not specifically directed to children under the age required by applicable laws. If required, the app will request consent before serving personalized advertising.

### Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information handled by the application. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.
